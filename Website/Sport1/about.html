About Us

OneSport organizes sports activities which will be held in the Philippines which unites every Filipino athletes and each activities will be very memorable for everyone. OneSport gathers all athletes around the Philippines to join Sports Activities which consists of many sports like Basketball, Swimming and many more and there would be also corresponded rewards for every winners. Each activities can be seen and can be joined by any athletes
